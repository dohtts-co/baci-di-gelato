Our gelato

Not just ice cream

There's a moment — the first spoonful — where everything else disappears. That dense, silky texture. The flavour that arrives all at once and stays.

Gelato is made with more milk than cream, so it's naturally lower in fat — and with less air churned in, every scoop is richer and more intense than ordinary ice cream. Ours uses only natural ingredients, shaped by recipes passed down through generations and guided by our mentor, Jonathan Susca.

We're Erin and Jonathan. We started Baci Di Gelato because the best things are sweeter when they're shared.

— Erin & Jonathan
